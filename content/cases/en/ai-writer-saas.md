## Project Background

After the explosion of social media, the demand for content has surged, but the efficiency of producing high-quality content remains low. The team saw an opportunity: using AI to boost writing efficiency while ensuring quality.

## Product Form

An AI writing assistant integrated with GPT-4, supporting long-form generation, style adjustment, and SEO optimization. Core functions: topic generation, outline expansion, style unification, and multi-platform publishing.

## Implementation Process

**January 2024**: Beta version launched  
**April 2024**: Official version released  
**August 2024**: Users exceeded 5,000  
**December 2024**: Monthly revenue reached ¥80,000

## Monetization Model

Subscription + Pay-as-you-go:
- Free version: 3,000 words/month
- Professional version: ¥99/month, 100,000 words/month
- Team version: ¥299/month, unlimited words + collaboration features

Average Revenue Per User (ARPU): ¥180/month, conversion rate 15%.

## Core Insights

1. **Freemium Strategy**: The free version lowers the barrier to entry and accumulates users.
2. **Clear Demand**: Solving the dual needs of "speed" and "quality."
3. **Community Operations**: Building a user community to promote word-of-mouth.
