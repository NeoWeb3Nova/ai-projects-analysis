## Project Background

Founded by two former Alibaba Cloud engineers, they identified the high cost and slow response issues in traditional customer service systems. Enterprise customer service costs account for 15-20% of operating costs, often with a poor user experience.

## Product Form

A smart customer service system based on GPT-4, supporting multi-turn dialogue, knowledge base Q&A, and sentiment recognition. Core functions include: automated response, human handover, data analysis, and learning optimization.

## Implementation Process

**March 2024**: MVP launched, tested with single client  
**June 2024**: Secured seed funding  
**September 2024**: Users reached 100  
**December 2024**: Monthly revenue reached ¥50,000

## Monetization Model

Subscription-based model:
- Basic Edition: ¥299/month, includes 500 dialogues
- Professional Edition: ¥899/month, includes 5,000 dialogues + custom training
- Enterprise Edition: ¥2,999/month, unlimited calls + private deployment

Avg Order Value: ¥450/month, 85% retention rate, CAC ¥80/user.

## Core Insights

1. **Clear Entry Point**: Solving real business pain points (cost + experience).
2. **Keep it Simple**: Focus on customer service scenarios without blind expansion.
3. **Tiered Pricing**: Tiered pricing to meet the needs of different scale enterprises.
4. **Continuous Optimization**: Rapid iteration based on feedback.
